About Me

Founder of Impact Driven Businesses

I am a visionary entrepreneur committed to creating platforms that transform lives through financial education, Gospel truth, and strategic investments.

With ventures spanning financial literacy, biblical teaching, investment management, and cross-sector business development, my mission is to empower individuals and communities to achieve both financial freedom and spiritual growth.

Through FinMastery, I help people break free from poverty mindsets. With The Gospel Truths, I spread sound biblical teaching. Through Ads Bego, PERUS Group, and PIT Fund, I create opportunities for wealth creation and economic development.
